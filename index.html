[ FAQs ]

What is octathon?

A octathon is a social coding event that brings computer programmers and other interested people together to improve upon or build a new software program.

Who can participate?

Everyone is welcome to participate in this event. While experience coding and programming is a huge plus, teams will also need people with strong presentation skills and brilliant ideas.

Why should I participate?

Octathon is a great way to learn new skills that will be useful in your every day work as a researcher. Beyond that,octathon are also an opportunity to meet.

What is the mode of event?

The event will be conducted in offline mode . All the participants have to come to college for participating in it.

Is there a maximum team size?

Yes , you can make a team of maximum 6 members .

How can I register in octathon ?

You can register in octathon by visiting official website and filling the required details.
