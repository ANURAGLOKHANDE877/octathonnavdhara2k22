ANTI-DRONE SYSTEM
1. To design solution architecture for the Anti-drone System for MP Police to thwart the mission of the rogue drone. Anti-drone systems detect and or intercept unwanted drones and unmanned aerial vehicles (UAVs). They are deployed to protect areas such as airports, critical infrastructure, large public spaces such as stadiums, and military installations and battlefields. Following are some of the drone monitoring techniques to thwart the mission of a rogue drone. ⦁ Radio Frequency (RF) Analyzers ⦁ Acoustic Sensors (Microphones) ⦁ Optical Sensors (Cameras), ⦁ Radar
